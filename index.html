/ABOUT

Hey! I'm Sathnindu.

I'm a software engineer based in Sri Lanka, currently leading backend engineering and DevOps for FinTech products at Arimac Digital — including PayLater, a Qatar-founded Buy Now Pay Later platform.

My focus is framework engineering: not writing applications, but the layer beneath them. The research form of that work became a first-author paper at ICCTA 2026 in Vienna. Its open-source form is Wolfigs — developer tooling engineered in Sri Lanka and published globally.
